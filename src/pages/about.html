About MiTHAI

Reimagining how we document culinary creativity.

The Story

"Mithai" means sweets in Urdu. This project was born from a simple frustration: recipes are never really finished. We swap ingredients, adjust cooking times, and try new flavors, but most of those experiments never get written down.

Most recipe apps treat recipes like static instructions you just follow. MiTHAI is different. It lets you create "Remixes" to track your variations, rate what worked, and watch your recipes get better over time.

What It Does

MiTHAI helps you keep track of your cooking experiments without the mess of scattered notes or trying to remember what you did last time. Store your original recipes and every variation you try.

  • Create recipes with ingredients and instructions
  • Make "Remixes" that save only what you changed
  • Rate recipes each time you make them
  • See your cooking data through charts and stats
  • Export or delete your recipes anytime

Every recipe is a starting point. Each remix links back to show what you changed. Over time, you'll see which tweaks worked and which versions became your favorites.

Why It Matters

Cooking is experimental, but most successful tweaks never get written down. MiTHAI fixes that with one simple goal: Keep the good ideas and learn from the rest.

  • Stop repeating the same mistakes
  • Find your best versions quickly
  • Actually improve your cooking over time
  • Share recipes you know work

Your recipes grow with you. They're not stuck on paper or in your head.

Features

  • Create recipes and remixes
  • Upload images or pull from TheMealDB
  • Rate recipes with a 5-star system
  • Filter by cuisine, meal type, or ingredient
  • Step-by-step cooking mode
  • Charts showing what you cook most
  • Everything saves locally in your browser

Works on your phone, tablet, or computer. No account needed.

How It's Built

  • HTML5
  • CSS3 with Bootstrap
  • Vanilla JavaScript
  • LocalStorage for data
  • TheMealDB API for recipe images
  • Chart.js for visualizations

Built to meet WCAG AA accessibility standards with proper heading structure, keyboard navigation, and high contrast throughout.
