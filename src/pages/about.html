About MiTHAI

Reimagining how we document culinary creativity.

The Story

"Mithai" means sweets in Urdu. This project was born out of a simple problem: recipes are rarely static. We tweak them, we change ingredients based on what's in our fridge, and we adjust cooking times based on our appliances.

Most recipe apps treats recipes as "read-only" instructions. MithAI treats them as living documents—encouraging you to create "Remixes" (variations) and track which version tasted the best.

Version Control for Food

Just like code has branches, your recipes have variations. Track every change you make.

Data-Driven Cooking

Visualize your cooking habits. See which cuisines you cook the most and analyze your ratings.
